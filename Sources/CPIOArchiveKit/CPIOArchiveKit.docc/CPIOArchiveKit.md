# ``CPIOArchiveKit``

A simple Swift package for reading and writing [`cpio`](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cpio) archives. Inspired by [go-cpio](https://github.com/cavaliercoder/go-cpio).

To get started, go to ``CPIOArchiveKit/CPIOArchive``
